# Source & Stakeholder Map — Crude Reserves & Restocking Intelligence

The people, institutions, and companies that matter for the manager's objective (country-level crude-reserve coverage + restocking outlook, Hormuz-2026 context). Organised by tier. **F** = free · **$** = paid/subscription · **Data** = hard numbers · **View** = forecast/colour.

> Honest note: this is the bench to *wire up or subscribe to*. My own live access remains limited (egress); naming ≠ having pulled them. The point is to tell you exactly where the authoritative data and views live.

## Tier 1 — Official / multilateral data (the reserve-coverage gold standard)
| Source | What it gives | Access |
|---|---|---|
| **IEA** — Oil Market Report (OMR), *Oil stocks of IEA countries*, Monthly Oil Data Service | THE days-of-net-import-cover series; OECD stocks; balances | $ (OMR), some F |
| **EIA** (US) — Weekly Petroleum Status Report, STEO, *This Week in Petroleum*, SPR data | US SPR + commercial stocks (weekly), price/import series, forecasts | **F** |
| **OPEC Secretariat** — Monthly Oil Market Report (MOMR) | OPEC supply, demand, OECD stocks view | **F** |
| **JODI** (Joint Organisations Data Initiative, hosted by IEF) | Stocks + production incl. **non-OECD/OPEC** — the only multilateral non-OECD stock source | **F** (lagged ~2mo) |
| **IMF** — *PortWatch* (with Oxford), commodity prices, Article IV | Chokepoint transit monitoring; macro context | **F** |
| **Energy Institute** — Statistical Review of World Energy (ex-BP) | Annual reserves/consumption baselines | **F** |
| **Eurostat / Euroilstock** | EU member oil stocks | **F** |
| National agencies | country-level strategic + commercial stocks | mixed |
| → US DOE/SPR Office · Japan **METI/ANRE, JOGMEC, PAJ** · Korea **KNOC, KEEI** · India **PPAC** · Germany **EBV/BAFA** · France **SAGESS/CPSSP** · Italy **OCSIT/GME** · Spain **CORES** · UK **DESNZ** · China **NBS/Customs** (sparse) · Saudi **GASTAT** (no stock disclosure) | | |

## "Wire these first" — biggest fidelity gain per cost
1. **Free, now:** EIA (US verified), JODI (non-OECD), IMF PortWatch (Hormuz), OPEC MOMR — closes most of the *verified* gap for free.
2. **One paid feed if budget:** **IEA OMR** (the per-country days-of-cover the brief is built on) **or** **Kpler/Vortexa** (the "today" flow/inventory engine). These two are where the real per-country precision lives.
3. **Geopolitics view:** RBC (Croft) + Rapidan — for the Hormuz scenario calls.
4. **Producer ground truth:** the `satellite/` pipeline on free Sentinel (already built) or Kayrros/Ursa if buying.
